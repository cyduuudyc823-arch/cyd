DOGGO GO

Community Care Project

A trust-driven, low-barrier, non-purely-commercial mutual assistance platform connecting pet owners and service providers.

01 — Context & Opportunity

Market Landscape

China’s pet industry is rapidly expanding. However, many pet owners face recurring “walking dilemmas” due to overtime work, business travel, or unpredictable schedules.

Core Insight

Marketplace data indicates strong unmet demand. Since June, over 105,000 posts offering dog-walking services and 27,000 posts seeking walkers have been published.

DAILY DEMAND > 5,000 LISTINGS

Positioning

A trust-driven, low-barrier, non-purely-commercial mutual assistance platform.

02 — Competitor Analysis

Platform Core Strength Gap
Rover Mature review system; pet insurance included High background check fee ($35); high entry barrier
PetPie Clean interface; professional standards Long review process; opaque specialist allocation
PetHelper Feature-rich; integrates idle trading Weak guarantee system; complex interface
Opportunity: Build a lightweight but trust-intensive platform with transparent onboarding and simplified interaction.

Name: Zhang Wu

Age: 45 / Chengdu

Job: Instructor

Pet: Samoyed

Scenario

Ms. Zhang plans to dine with friends. Her dog is home alone. She needs a reliable person to walk her dog and return it safely so her pet is cared for while she's away.

Expectations

  • Short-term home care or walking.
  • Certified/vetted providers.
  • Detailed service guarantees.

Pain Points

  • Trust established with strangers.
  • Fear of accidents/illness.
  • Property/Home security.

Solutions

  • Platform-borne Insurance.
  • Verified health/vaccination certs.
  • Simplified Training + Assessment.

Name: Wu Yiyi

Age: 20 / Nanjing

Job: Undergraduate

Pet: Cat/Dog Lover

Scenario

An outgoing student who loves pets. On weekends, she wants to help others walk dogs to relax. Finds existing platforms too "business-like".

Expectations

  • Connect with other pet lovers.
  • Purely enjoy dog interaction.
  • A way to relax on weekends.

Pain Points

  • Handling different breeds.
  • Registration is too complex.
  • Owners' lack of trust in youth.

Solutions

  • Tiered pricing based on exp.
  • Video training boost.
  • Non-commercial app design.

Name: Yang Chi
